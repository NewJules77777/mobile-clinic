About the Mobile Clinic Approach

The mobile clinic approach is a healthcare delivery model that involves bringing essential healthcare services, including Adolescent Sexual Reproductive Health (ASRH) services, directly to communities. These mobile clinics are equipped to provide a range of services such as counseling, education, testing, and treatment, particularly targeting underserved or remote areas where access to healthcare facilities is limited

Our Mission

The mission of the mobile clinic for ASRH in Rwanda is to improve the health and well-being of adolescents by providing comprehensive and youth-friendly ASRH services directly to their communities. By bringing these services closer to where adolescents live, we aim to increase access and reduce barriers to care.

Our Objectives

  • Provide Comprehensive ASRH Services: Offer a wide range of services including counseling, education, family planning, STI testing and treatment, and general healthcare to address the holistic needs of adolescents.
  • Reduce Adolescent Pregnancies and STIs: Through education, counseling, and access to contraception, the mobile clinic aims to reduce the incidence of unintended pregnancies and sexually transmitted infections (STIs) among adolescents.
  • Improve Awareness about ASRH: Conduct community outreach and educational programs to raise awareness about ASRH issues, promote healthy behaviors, and reduce stigma surrounding reproductive health.
